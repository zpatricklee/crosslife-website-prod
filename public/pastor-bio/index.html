PASTOR BIO

Rev. Isaac Mun is the Lead Pastor of Crosslife Christian Fellowship. He is passionate about preaching the Word of God and shepherding people toward a deeper life in Christ.

Isaac earned his Master of Divinity from Talbot School of Theology and a Teaching Elder in the Presbyterian Church in America (PCA).

He and his wife, Tina, have three dogs and enjoy daily walks together. They are thankful to be part of the Crosslife family and excited to see how God continues to work in and through the church.
